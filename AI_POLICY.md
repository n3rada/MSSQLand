# AI Policy

MSSQLand was built entirely by humans. The [`AI.md`](AI.md) and [`DEVELOPMENT.md`](DEVELOPMENT.md) files exist to give AI tools enough context to assist contributors without working against the existing architecture.

## AI is Welcome

AI-assisted contributions are accepted, subject to the following rules:

1. **Understand what you submit.** If you cannot explain what your changes do and how they interact with the existing architecture without AI assistance, do not submit them. Unreviewed AI output is not a contribution.
2. **Disclose AI usage.** State the tool(s) used and the extent of assistance directly in the pull request description.
3. **Review before posting.** Issues, discussions, and PR descriptions may involve AI assistance, but all content must be reviewed and edited by a human before submission. Strip the noise, keep what is accurate and relevant.
4. **No AI-generated media** (images, diagrams, screenshots, audio, video, etc.).
5. **Repeated low-effort AI submissions will result in a permanent contribution ban.**

These rules apply to outside contributors. Maintainers apply their own judgment.
